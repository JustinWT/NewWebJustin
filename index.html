About Me

I grew up in the United States of America. I was born in Florida and lived in many different cities in Florida. I spent my middle school years in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania and then went to High School in Atlanta, Georgia. After high school I returned to Florida to attend Florida State University. I studied Criminology, Politcal Science, and History. Then I attended Law School at Florida State University. I worked as a lobbyist and then in the legal department of Coca-Cola in Atlanta, Georgia. I moved to Barcelona, Spain to change my life style and get out of corporate America.My entire life I have played all American team sports and love outdoor sports as well. Now that I am out of the corporate world I have time every month to do the following activities:

  • Trekking
  • Mountain Biking
  • Surfing
  • Skiing/Snowboarding
  • Swimming
  • Climbing
